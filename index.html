Washington Weightlifting

Washington Weightlifting is the Local Weightlifting Committee for the state of Washington.

The olympic sport of weightlifting involves two competition lifts: the Snatch and the Clean & Jerk. Weightlifting can be practiced by men and women of all ages to improve strength, power, speed, flexibility, and confidence!

LWC Annual Meeting

Imperial Crossfit
December 20, 2014
2pm - 4pm

On December 20, 2014, we'll be hosting our Annual Meeting at the at Imperial Crossfit in Kent from 2:00pm to 4:00pm located at 702 W Meeker St, Kent, WA 98032 - Phone: (206) 234-8076. Will be electing new official for President, Vice-President, Secretary, Treasure and we adding two new positions Sanction Officer and Athlete Representative.

Open Nomination Period: People can self-nominate or be nominated for an LWC position by November 30. Voting will be done at the LWC Annual Meeting. Term limits for Office will be until December 2016. After that, a 4 year term will be from December 2016-December 2020.

Future officers should meet in person at least 3 times (April, July, December) within one calendar year in addition to regular email contact with other officers on issues as it comes up.

Attached are the bylaws draft and Meet Sanctions Guidelines addendum for review and discussion at the annual meeting. Joe Quinn: Is the current Interim President of WA state LWC and will be acting as primary point of contact for any issues between now and the December meeting. Joe will notify USAW of any changes. One of the main goals is to get the By Laws approved at the December 20 meeting.

Meeting Location: Imperial Crossfit in Kent will be used as the location to hold the LWC Meeting from 2:00 pm-4:00pm. Please show up 15 minutes prior.

Update Bylaws with the inclusion of two additional positions to committee: Brings total to 5 governing boards members up for election in December.

Two new positions to LWC governing board:
SANCTION OFFICER: The Sanction Officer shall be responsible solely for the execution of approved sanction requests from the LWC President/ back-up officer. The Sanction Officer will be in contact with USAW's National Office to get a sanction approval number and provide it back to the requester and file other necessary paperwork as necessary. The SANCTION OFFICER will provide feedback to the Executive Board on any sanction issues that may arise.

ATHLETE REPRESENTATIVE: The Athlete Representatives shall be invited to attend all meetings of the Executive Board; shall fully participate therein and perform such other duties as designated by the Executive Board. Specifically, the Athlete Representative(s) shall be a sounding board between the Athlete and the Executive Board, bringing to the Executive Board the problems and desires of our athletes.
